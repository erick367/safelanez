About Safe Lane Driving School

Welcome to Safe Lane Driving School, Sheffield's trusted choice for learning to drive. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, patient and friendly driving tuition tailored to your individual needs.

Our mission is simple: to help you become a safe, confident and responsible driver for life, not just to pass your test. We pride ourselves on our high pass rates and the positive feedback we receive from our students.

We use modern, dual-controlled vehicles and employ the latest teaching techniques to ensure you learn effectively in a relaxed and supportive environment. We offer both Manual and Automatic lessons.

  • Manual & Automatic Dual-Controlled Cars
  • Fully Qualified & DVSA Approved Instructors
  • Patient, Friendly & Encouraging Approach
  • Flexible Lesson Times (Evenings & Weekends available)
  • Excellent First-Time Pass Rates
