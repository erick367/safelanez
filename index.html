Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book my first lesson?

Simply click the Book Now button or visit our Booking page to fill out the form. We'll get in touch to confirm your details and arrange your first session.

Do you offer both manual and automatic lessons?

Yes! We offer both manual and automatic driving lessons. Just let us know your preference when booking.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all areas of Sheffield. If you're unsure, just contact us to we'd be more than happy to clarify.

How do I pay for lessons?

Payment can be made by bank transfer or cash. Details will be provided when your booking is confirmed.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Please give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a lesson to avoid a cancellation fee.
